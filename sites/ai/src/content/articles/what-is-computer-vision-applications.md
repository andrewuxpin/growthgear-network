## What is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence that trains computers to interpret and understand visual information from digital images, videos, and real-world environments. It combines machine learning algorithms, deep learning neural networks, and image processing techniques to enable machines to "see" and make decisions based on visual data.

The technology works by:
- **Image acquisition**: Capturing visual data through cameras or sensors
- **Pre-processing**: Cleaning and preparing images for analysis
- **Feature extraction**: Identifying key visual elements and patterns
- **Classification and detection**: Recognizing objects, faces, or specific features
- **Decision making**: Taking action based on visual analysis
